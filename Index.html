About FinanceTracker

FinanceTracker is designed to help students take control of their finances. Managing money as a student can be challenging, and our goal is to make it simple and intuitive.

With easy-to-use budgeting tools, transaction tracking, and clear visualizations, you can understand your spending habits and make better financial decisions.

  • Track all your income and expenses in one place
  • Set budgets for different categories and monitor your spending
  • Search and filter transactions to find what you need
  • Visualize your financial health with clear dashboards
